# Pilot Protocol

## Cohort
- 30 to 60 learners
- One defined year group or transition point

## Duration
- 6 to 8 weeks

## Cases
- 6 to 10 clinically reviewed cases
- Formative use only

## Recruitment
- Faculty-authorised invitation
- Optional unless formally embedded and approved

## Consent
- Educational-use notice
- Separate research participation decision where required

## Measures
- Pre/post performance task
- Student usability and usefulness ratings
- Educator scoring sample
- Technical incident monitoring

## Technical Monitoring
- Completion rates
- AI fallback rates
- Technical failure rates
- Small-sample warnings

## Adverse Event Reporting
- Safety concern escalation
- Student distress or professionalism concern reporting
- Technical incident log

## Analysis Plan
- Descriptive pilot analysis only
- Educator-agreement sampling
- No claims of statistical significance from small samples

## Stop Criteria
- Repeated safety-critical content defects
- Unacceptable privacy/security risk
- Persistent technical failure that disrupts learning
