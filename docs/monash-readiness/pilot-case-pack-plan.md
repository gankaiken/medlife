# Pilot Case-Pack Plan

## Recommended Learner Group
- Candidate target: Year 2 to Year 3 transition and early-clinical learners.
- Neutral Medlife stage labels:
  - `transition_to_clinical_learning`
  - `early_clinical`

## Proposed Initial Pack
- `case-headache-001`: focused history, concern exploration, safe reassurance.
- `case-cough-003`: respiratory red flags, escalation judgement, safety-netting.
- `case-anemia-002`: fatigue workup, investigation planning, follow-up safety.
- Candidate additions for later review only:
  - Chest pain
  - Abdominal pain
  - Dysuria
  - Hypertension follow-up
  - Diabetes follow-up
  - Mental-health presentation

## Educational Design Intention
- 6 to 10 cases total.
- Formative use only.
- Mix of primary-care communication, reasoning, and red-flag recognition.
- Short cases suitable for briefing, attempt, reflection, and educator sampling.

## Readiness Gates For Any New Case
- Medical author review
- Clinical reviewer approval
- Curriculum mapping review
- Reference verification
- Safety review
- Pilot approval

## Cases Must Remain
- `development_only` until all required review areas are complete.
