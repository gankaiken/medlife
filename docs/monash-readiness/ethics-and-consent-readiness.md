# Ethics And Consent Readiness

## Clear Boundaries
- Educational use is not automatically research.
- Product analytics are not automatically research.
- Research participation requires a separate decision path.

## Current Readiness Direction
- Educational-use notice can be acknowledged inside the platform.
- Research participation can be stored separately from normal educational use.
- Withdrawal status and deidentified research IDs can be represented technically.

## External Decisions Still Required
- Whether formal ethics review is required.
- Final approved participant information wording.
- Final approved consent and withdrawal workflow.
- Whether the pilot is optional or embedded within a formally approved teaching activity.

## Non-Claims
- Technical implementation does not equal ethics approval.
- Platform participation must not be presented as compulsory unless formally approved.
- Publication and dissemination require authorised review and appropriate consent.
